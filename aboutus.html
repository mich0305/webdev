About Us

Welcome to Kape Tayo sa Bahay! We are passionate about crafting the perfect cup of coffee for every coffee lover. Our coffee shop was founded on the idea of bringing people together over a warm and comforting cup of coffee, just like home.

With carefully selected beans and expert brewing techniques, we aim to serve high-quality, flavorful coffee that leaves a lasting impression. Whether you love espresso, iced coffee, or ice-blended drinks, we have something special for you.

Come visit us and experience the warmth and aroma of Kape Tayo sa Bahay!
